About

Welcome to Lush Bakes

Hello, I'm Lisa, a passionate and self-taught baker based in Parson Drove, a small village in the Cambridgehire area. From the cozy corners of my kitchen, I craft delectable creations that add a sprinkle of sweetness to life's celebrations.

As the founder of Lush Bakes, I specialize in handcrafting bespoke birthday cakes, celebration cakes, cupcakes, and delightful cookie cakes. Each creation is infused with love, creativity, and a pinch of magic, aiming to make your special moments even more memorable.

If you're looking to elevate your occasion with a sweet touch, or if you have any inquiries, I will be delighted to assist you!
